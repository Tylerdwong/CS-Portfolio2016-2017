Hardware

Introduction

Welcome to the Hardware page! This page you will learn about the essential parts of the computer and what each parts do.

Motherboard

The motherboard is one of the most essential parts of the computer and holds the most crucial parts of the computer together and keeps it all connected together. They are also specifically designed for certain types of processors and RAM. They also provide USB ports and ports for connecting video and sound cards. Motherboards can also be known as the "brains" of the computer. Overall, motherboards are basically just there to provide power for individual components and to provide connection. But a motherboard cannot run without a processor.


CPU(Central Processing Unit)

The CPU is basically the brains of the computer where most of the calculations take place or is also known as the processor or microprocessor. The CPU is also responsible for executing a sequence of stored instructions also known as a program. The CPU helps the user or us do things like typing, reading emails, and even making a photo album.

RAM(Random Access Memory)

RAM also known as random access memory it’s a type of computer memory that can be accessed randomly. It’s also the most common type of memory found in places such as computers, and other devices like printers. There are 2 main types of RAM, there is DRAM(Dynamic Random Access Memory) and SPAM(Static Random Access Memory)

Hard Drive

Everything you keep on your computer is basically all part of the hard drive. A hard drive is a big storage place that can hold storge like gigabytes and most of the time it's over 100 gigabytes on the hard drive. It houses the hard disk which stores all your files and folders. The data is stored on the drive magnetically which means it stays on the drive even when it is turned off. The term hard drive is basically short for hard disk drive. However all of these terms are usually related to the same thing the place where your data is stored.
